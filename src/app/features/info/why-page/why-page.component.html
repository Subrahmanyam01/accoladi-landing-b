Why We Do It

Somebody Opened a Door for Us.
Now We're Holding It Open for Others.

Accoladi began with a simple truth:

Talent grows faster when someone believes in it.

Founder Dr. Randall Bayne was a first-generation college student whose music teachers showed him how to turn talent into opportunity.

As a teacher, he passed that knowledge to his own students.

As a college dean, he saw how many talented young artists never received the same guidance.

So he built the system he wished every student had.
